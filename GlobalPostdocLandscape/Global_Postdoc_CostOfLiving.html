Data Sources

  • Salaries: NIH NRSA FY2025 ($62,652/yr = $5,221/mo, NOT-OD-25-105); Research Ireland 2025–2026 New PostDoc P1 (€47,273/yr); SNSF Postdoc.Mobility (~CHF 7,800/mo); DFG TVL-E13 Stufe 1 (~€4,759/mo, 2025/26); UKRI 2025 Grade 6 min (~£40,497/yr); FCT 2025 junior researcher (~€2,950/mo); FWF; Academy of Finland; VR Sweden (~SEK 42,000/mo); JSPS PD ¥362k/mo; NRF Korea ~KRW 3M/mo; CNRS; A*STAR/NRF Singapore ~SGD 5,900/mo; NSERC PDF CAD 70,000/yr; IIT Delhi/DST-SERB (~INR 82k+HRA); CONAHCYT ~MXN 32k/mo; NRF South Africa ~ZAR 35k/mo
  • MSCA-PF (2023–2025 WP, 2025 call): living allowance €5,990/mo × country correction coefficient + €710 mobility. CCCs: Switzerland 163.8%, UK 141.7%, Norway ~145%, Ireland 110%, Sweden 112%, Germany 101.2%, Spain 95.6%, Portugal 86%, Greece 80%, Poland 77%, Turkey 68%. Switzerland MSCA-eligible from Jan 2025 (transitional arrangement; SERI backstops funding if formal association agreement not signed by grant date). Amounts converted at Feb 2026 EUR/USD = 1.18.
  • Rents: Numbeo 2025 studio apartment (not 1-bedroom) near university. Cross-checked: Daft.ie (Dublin), Rightmove (UK), Immoscout24 (DE), Idealista (ES/PT), Magicbricks (Delhi). European costs reflect EUR/GBP/CHF strengthening vs USD (~7–15%) since 2023–2024.
  • Turkey: Horizon Europe associated country (TUBITAK NCP confirmed). MSCA-PF CCC ~68% → €5,990×0.68+€710=€4,783/mo≈$5,644 at EUR/USD 1.18. TUBITAK 2218 fellowship TRY 43,500/mo (~$1,175 at ₺37/USD, 2025). TUBITAK 2218 stipend is tax-exempt under Turkish law (fellowship income is not classified as employment wage; no income tax deducted). Employed postdocs join SGK from day 1 (work permit), providing universal healthcare incl. basic dental; SGK covers ~99% of population.
  • Tax status by program (verified 2025): Tax-exempt: TUBITAK 2218 (Turkey — fellowship not classified as wage income); JSPS overseas fellowship (Japan — explicitly "not employment-based wages, exempted from taxation" per JSPS guidelines). Taxable: NIH NRSA (US — "subject to federal and state income tax," reported on Form 1099, per NIAID); NSERC PDF / Banting (Canada — explicitly taxable since 2010 CRA ruling, Box 105 T4A); UKRI, DFG TVL-E13, Research Ireland (employment contracts, fully taxable as salary); SNSF Postdoc.Mobility (Swiss income tax applies); MSCA-PF (taxed as employment income in host country, at host-country rates). Japan domestic JSPS PD: classified as 雑所得 (miscellaneous income), not salary — no source withholding, no social insurance contributions; effective rate ~13% after ¥480k basic deduction, lower than the ~24% applied to salaried institutional postdocs.
  • Dental affordability: scores reflect cost of routine dental care relative to postdoc salary. Sources: NHS dental charges (UK), SGK Turkey, NHIS Korea/Japan, Numbeo dental procedures, Expatistan dental index 2025. Low-cost leaders: Turkey, India, Mexico (medical tourism destinations). High OOP: US, Switzerland, Ireland (no adult public dental plan).
  • Inflation: World Bank WDI 2025; IMF World Economic Outlook Oct 2025; 5-year averages 2020–2025
  • Healthcare: WHO World Health Statistics 2024; Numbeo Healthcare Index 2025; OECD Health at a Glance 2024. Scores calibrated for foreign postdoctoral researchers on employment contracts.
  • Living Wage — methodology: All figures are net monthly cost floors (what you need to spend, not earn) — enabling direct, fair comparison with postdoc net salary. US cities: MIT Living Wage Calculator 2025 "Typical Expenses" table (1 adult, 0 children) ÷ 12, which explicitly excludes income taxes. MIT's headline living wage (gross) was NOT used. All other cities: (rent + food + transport + utilities) × 1.30 buffer, rounded to nearest $50, covering internet, clothing, personal care and misc. Japan +$250 for mandatory NHI + pension self-payment by JSPS fellows. Turkey: national benchmark retained. Prior EU figures were inflated gross wage benchmarks and have been corrected to net. All USD at Feb 2026 rates.
  • Industry postdoc: Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, LinkedIn salary data; representative pharma/biotech hubs 2025–2026. USD values use Feb 2026 exchange rates.

All figures are estimates for comparative purposes. Verify current rates with funding bodies, institutions, and national tax authorities before making career decisions.
